Keep Every Google Slides Presentation on Time

Slides Timer adds a smart, per-slide countdown overlay for Google Slides. Give it a total time and a slide range; it divides time evenly, lets you add/remove time per slide, and dynamically redistributes unused or overrun time during your talk.

Runs locally No analytics Read-only Slides scope Draggable overlay

How it works

  • Select a slide range (e.g., slides 1–10) and a total time (e.g., 10 minutes).
  • Optionally add per-slide overrides (e.g., 3: +2, 5: -1, or 7: =3 minutes).
  • Unused time rolls forward to later slides; overruns are subtracted from the remaining ones.
  • To validate your range, the extension can read the active deck’s slide count via the Google Slides API (presentations.readonly). No content is modified.

Privacy at a glance

  • No personal data collection. Settings are stored locally in Chrome storage.
  • Google Slides API used only to read slide counts when you opt in.

Privacy Policy

Slides Timer is a Chrome extension that runs locally in your browser.

What we collect

  • No personal data is collected.
  • Settings you configure (total time, slide range, per-slide overrides) are stored locally via Chrome storage.
  • When you opt in, the extension uses the Google Slides API with the scope presentations.readonly to read the number of slides in the active deck to validate your inputs. No slide content is downloaded or stored, and nothing is written back to Slides.

Sharing

No data is sold or shared with third parties.
